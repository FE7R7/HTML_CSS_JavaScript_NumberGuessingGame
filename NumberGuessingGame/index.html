Guessing Game

The rules of the game:

The rules of the game are as follows:

  1. Using the dropdown, select how many goes you get
  2. Click on "Generate Random"
  3. A new random number will be generated between 1 and 100
  4. Enter your answer into the textbox
  5. Click "Submit Guess"
  6. If your answer is too low or too high then an image will be displayed to let you know
  7. If you use all of your guesses without guessing the correct number then you loose
